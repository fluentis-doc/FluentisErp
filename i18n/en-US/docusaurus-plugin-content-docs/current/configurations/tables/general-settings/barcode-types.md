The table opens via the path **Tables > General Settings > Barcode types** and allows for the encoding of different item classes and the grouping of items.

It allows you to insert new records or search for existing ones to view, modify, or delete them.

The form consists of a filter area and a results area. Once all desired filters are set, simply click the **Search** button to display the results within the results grid.

To insert new records, you need to click in the upper grid on the first empty row or press the **New** button.

For anything not detailed in this document regarding the common functionality of forms, please refer to the following link [Common features, buttons, and fields](/docs/guide/common).
