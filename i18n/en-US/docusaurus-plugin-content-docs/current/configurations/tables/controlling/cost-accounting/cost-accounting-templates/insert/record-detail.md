In 'Record Detail' section it is possible to indicate a list of analytic detail accounts to be automatically proposed in the analytic records, created with template in use. These detail accounts will be proposed (with the related notes inserted into the specific field: it is not possible to manage template parametric codes) only in case that template has 'Value' and analytic account has associated a general ledger account on chart of accounts.
