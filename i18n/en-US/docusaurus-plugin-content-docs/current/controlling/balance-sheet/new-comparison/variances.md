It shows the details of compared reclassified balance sheets data. It is possible to view the variances among different reclassifications (as percentage of the previous column or as percentage of a specific fix column) and to view detail data of different reclassification levels (that is the detail of detail accounts, CC, PC, job orders that totalize the viewed node).

RIBBON BAR: it represents the Form menu, that is the area in which it is possible to perform actions linked to the procedure in use. The list of features is the following:



| Function | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Export Excel | It exports in excel data of variances viewed. |
