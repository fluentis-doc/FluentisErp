In **Fluentis ERP**, quality management is represented by all those procedures aimed at providing concrete organizational support both in the context of product realization or service delivery, as well as in managing staff engaged in achieving the objectives of the quality policy.

The Quality Management System aims to record and manage activities related to the specifications indicated in the ISO 9001 standard.   
In **Fluentis ERP**, Quality Management is natively integrated with the procedures of Purchasing Management, Production, Design, Logistics Management, and the Staff Management involved.
