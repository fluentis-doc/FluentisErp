---
title: Activate Fluent View for MES 
sidebar_position: 2
---

To use the Fluent view mode, designed to optimize the machine-side display of Fluentis MES, you need to follow these steps:

- Click on the gear icon on the left side of the screen 
- Open the settings
- Select the Fluent view

At this point, Fluentis will open with the Fluent view.
