It is possible to propose only predefined discounts taken by customer register and not those attributed to payment conditions or to certain items. Discounts, proposed on header of document, are reported in a new item line inserted on document. If after having inserted item lines, a new discount is inserted on header, it is not possible to duplicate this one in item lines, that have been already inserted.
