Formurile sunt:  [Natura tranzacției intrastat](/docs/configurations/tables/finance/nature-of-intrastat-transaction),  [Numerotare intrastat](/docs/configurations/tables/fluentis-numerations),  [Coduri nomenclatură](/docs/configurations/tables/finance/nomenclature-codes).
